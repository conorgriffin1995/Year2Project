CUSTOMER MANUAL

REGISTER:

At the top of the website in the navigation bar there is a button which allows users to register.

Click this button and it will bring you to a register form where the user will fill out their details.


Once the user fills in their details and clicks ‘Create Account’, they will be redirected to the ‘Login’ page where they will then be able to login in.

LOGIN:

Logging in is very much similar to registering. There is a ‘Login’ button beside the ‘Register’ button on the navigation bar.

When the login button is clicked the user will be redirected to a login form, where all the user needs to enter is their email and password.


After signing in, the user will be directed to the home page which will have slight differences to the home page before they were logged in. On the navigation bar the user will now see a logout button instead of a login button and the user will also see a button to view their previous orders.


The page will also give them a welcoming message with their name attached at the end instead of just the basic ‘Welcome’.


VIEW PRODUCTS:

In order for the user to view products they must click the drop down menu on the navigation bar.

After the button is clicked it will give the user 4 options to choose from.


The user can view the products by their respective category or just view a big list of all the products on the one page. Either button will bring the user to the products page where they can purchase from.

ADD PRODUCT TO BASKET:

Once on the products page the user will be able to see a list of products we have to offer. If any of these products catches their eye, they can simply click the small trolley symbol at the end of the product which will then put that product into their basket.

The product will stay in their basket even if they sign out, until the user decides to purchase this item it will stay in their basket. The user can click the shopping cart button on the navigation bar to view all items in their basket.


The items in the basket will be viewable in a neat organized table like so:


Users can add or remove to the quantity of an item in their basket by simply clicking the minus or plus symbols which mean ‘Decrement’ and ‘Increment’.


The user can simply in one click empty their basket by clicking the red button on the left which says ‘Empty Basket’. The user can also go back to shopping by clicking the blue button that says ‘Continue Shopping’ which will direct them back to the products page. The user can then decide to go through with the payment of their order by clicking the green button which says ‘Payment’.


PURCHASE YOUR ORDER:

After the user clicks the green ‘Payment’ button from the basket page they will be directed to another page which will have the following payment form:

The user will fill out this form and click another green button saying ‘Place Order’. After clicking this button the order will be processed and a summary/confirmation of the customer’s order will be displayed.


This confirmation page looks similar to the basket page but notice there are no buttons to edit the order as it has already been put through and paid for. The user can then click the blue ‘Finish’ button which will direct them back to the home page.

VIEW PREVIOUS ORDERS:

Once the customer has made 1 or more orders. These orders go into the customer’s history and are then viewable simply by clicking the ‘Previous Orders’ button on the navigation bar.

Once clicked, the user will be brought to a page which displays all the their previous orders in a neat organized table like so:


The user can see the exact time and date that their order was placed, what was on the order and the total price of the order. The user can also delete the order from their history.

HOW TO CONTACT US:

In a situation where a user would like to contact us, simply click the ‘Contact Us’ button from the navigation bar.


This button will then bring up many ways in which the user can come in contact with us:


The user can see our address, phone number, facebook page and also navigate through an interactive google map to see our whereabouts from a greater location. There is also a newsletter the user can sign up for to receive updates on our products and prices from time to time.

ABOUT US:

Simply click the 'About Us' button in the navigation bar to find out more about us!




ADMINISTRATOR MANUAL

VIEW PRODUCTS:

Administrators will automatically be brought to the products page when they sign in as this is where most of their functionality takes place.

ADD A PRODUCT

To add a product, scroll down to the bottom of the products page and there will be a blue button which says ‘Add Product’.

Once clicked, the admin will be brought to a page which will contain a form to enter in the details of a product.


Once filled out the admin can click the blue ‘Add Product’ button and it will save the product and redirect the admin to the products page.


The product can now be seen among the others. The ID of the product is automatically assigned. Previously there was no ID:1 so the new product was assigned this ID.


UPDATE A PRODUCT:

To update a product the admin must click the pencil button on the right side of the product

This button will direct them to the similar form as the ‘Add Product’ form.


You can see in this form how the products previous details are set and how they can be updated. When the admin clicks save they will be directed to the products page and the new changes can be seen on the product.

DELETE A PRODUCT:

To delete a product the admin simply has to click the bin button on the right side of the product.

Once clicked the admin will be given a warning asking them are they sure they want to delete the product from the database:


Click OK, and the product will disappear from the table of products.


VIEW ORDERS:

For the admin to view all of their customer’s previous orders they must go to the navigation bar and click the ‘All Orders’ button which will direct them to a page which contains a table of all the orders gone out to which customers(By ID)
